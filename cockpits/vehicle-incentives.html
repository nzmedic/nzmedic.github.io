What this model does not do (yet)
Included to make assumptions explicit and decision risks visible.
  • No behavioural feedback loops (e.g., channel mix and borrower quality changing over time).
  • Loss distribution is simplified; tail risk and correlation are not fully modelled.
  • No capital, liquidity, or operational capacity constraints included.
  • Assumes pricing, funding costs, and collections effectiveness remain stable.
